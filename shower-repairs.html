Shower repair

Shower repairs, leaks and pressure problems

A shower that drips, runs cold, loses pressure or leaks beneath the tray can involve very different repairs. Ken asks about the symptom and access before the estimate is produced.

Get a live estimateCall 020 7371 3333

Valve and cartridge faults

A shower that will not shut off or regulate temperature often points to the valve or cartridge, but parts availability depends on the manufacturer.

Waste and tray leaks

A leak beneath a shower tray can be simple if there is service access and far more involved if the waste is completely concealed.

Pressure problems

Low pressure can involve filters, restrictions, pumps or the way the hot and cold supplies are configured.

Frequently asked questions

Can you tell if the shower itself is leaking or the pipework?

Ken can help narrow the possibilities, but an on-site test is usually needed to confirm the exact source.

Will you remove tiles without asking?

No. Any significant opening-up or access work is discussed and priced before proceeding.
