Plumbing problems commonly found around W14

Leaks affecting the flat below

Bath wastes, shower seals, toilet connections and concealed pipework can allow water to travel before it appears on the ceiling below. The timing of the leak—during a shower, after flushing or continuously—helps narrow the source.

Toilet cistern faults in compact bathrooms

Running fill valves, worn flush seals and concealed cistern mechanisms are frequent small jobs. A photograph of the flush plate or inside the cistern is more useful than simply saying the toilet is broken.

Loft tanks, pumps and converted upper floors

Loft conversions can add shower pumps, tanks and long pipe runs. Noise, air and low pressure should be diagnosed as a system problem rather than assuming the pump alone has failed.

Local shop and landlord maintenance

North End Road and the surrounding streets include independent premises and rental flats where a failed toilet or active leak needs clear communication and practical scheduling.
