How it works

A clearer way to book a plumber.

Ken handles the first conversation. Our controlled estimator handles the price. The plumber confirms the real job on site.

01

Describe it

Tell Ken exactly what you can see, hear or feel. You do not need to know the plumbing terminology.

02

Answer a few questions

Ken asks about active leakage, access and location because those details can materially change the likely price.

03

Get the estimate

The estimate comes from the KPS pricing catalogue. AI is not allowed to make up the price.

04

Pay £75

Secure your attendance and diagnosis through SumUp. The £75 is credited against completed repair work.

05

Choose your preferred slot

After successful payment, the booking page asks for your preferred date and time window.

06

Confirm on site

The plumber checks the real fault and confirms the exact price before additional work is carried out.
