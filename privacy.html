Privacy

Privacy information.

Information you provide

When you use Ken or request a booking, we may collect the messages you send, postcode, estimate details, name, phone number, email address, payment reference and preferred appointment information.

Why we use it

We use this information to provide estimates, arrange visits, communicate about requested work, process payments, keep normal business records and improve the service.

Ken and AI processing

Where the AI connection is enabled, messages sent to Ken may be processed by our AI service to provide conversational guidance. The actual estimated price is calculated separately by the Kensington Plumbing Services pricing engine.

Payments

Online payments are processed by SumUp. This website does not need to store your full card details.

Contact

For privacy questions, email info@kensington.biz.
