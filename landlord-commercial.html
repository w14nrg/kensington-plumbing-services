Clear communication when the owner is not on site

Property plumbing often involves a tenant, owner, agent and contractor. The fault, access arrangements and authority for additional work should be clear before attendance. Photographs and concise updates help avoid confusion and repeated visits.

Typical landlord and business work

  • Toilet, tap and shower repairs in rental properties
  • Leaks affecting flats or neighbouring premises
  • Radiator valves, pumps, tanks and cylinders
  • Replacement fittings between tenancies
  • Small plumbing maintenance for shops, pubs and offices
  • Multi-item repair visits and defect lists
  • Photographic updates for owners and managers
  • Urgent attendance subject to location and availability

A practical service for smaller portfolios

Kensington Plumbing Services is suitable for owners and managers who value direct contact with the person attending. The service is not presented as a national facilities-management company. It is a local plumbing service with experience in occupied homes, managed housing and West London properties.

Commercial plumbing boundaries

The focus is everyday water, sanitary and maintenance plumbing for independent premises. Specialist industrial systems, major drainage work, gas appliances and regulated commercial heating work require appropriate specialist contractors.
