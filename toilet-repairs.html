Toilet repair

Toilet repairs without guessing what the fault might cost

Running cisterns, toilets that will not flush, slow filling, leaks around the pan and concealed cistern faults are among the most common jobs we attend.

Get a live estimateCall 020 7371 3333

Running or constantly filling toilets

Often caused by an inlet valve, float valve or flush-valve seal. Ken will ask what the water is doing before matching the job to the price range.

Toilet not flushing

Buttons, cables, levers, siphons and flush valves can all fail. The type of cistern and access determine the repair.

Leaks and pan connections

Water around the base or behind the toilet can involve the pan connector, cistern, flush pipe or supply. The plumber confirms the source before repair.

Frequently asked questions

Can you repair concealed cisterns?

Yes, where suitable service access exists. If tiles or boxing must be opened, that work is priced separately before proceeding.

Can you replace the whole toilet?

Yes. Ken includes a labour estimate for straightforward close-coupled toilet replacement; the sanitaryware cost is separate unless supplied by KPS.
