Tap repair and replacement

Tap repairs and replacements in Kensington and West London

Whether the problem is a persistent drip, a leak at the base or a mixer tap that needs replacing, access beneath the fitting is usually the biggest variable in the job.

Kitchen mixer taps

Straightforward kitchen mixer replacements are usually highly predictable when the pipe connections and isolation valves are accessible.

Basin taps

Basin and vanity units can make access easy or extremely tight. Ken adjusts the range when the job is awkward to reach.

Bath taps

Bath tap access varies widely. If a bath panel is removable the job may be straightforward; tiled-in or restricted access can increase the labour involved.

Frequently asked questions

Does the estimate include the new tap?

Unless specifically stated, Ken's replacement estimate covers labour and standard connection materials, not the cost of the selected tap.

Can you repair a dripping tap instead of replacing it?

Often yes. Washers, ceramic cartridges and valves can be replaced when suitable parts are available.
