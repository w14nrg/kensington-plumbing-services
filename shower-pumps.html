Shower pump diagnosis

Shower pump faults, diagnosis and replacement

A pump that is noisy, runs continuously or fails to start may have a pump fault, an air problem, a flow-switch issue or an unsuitable water supply.

Get a live estimateCall 020 7371 3333

Diagnosis first

Replacing a pump without understanding the cause can leave the original problem unresolved. We first confirm how the system is supplied and what the pump is doing.

Pump replacement

Where replacement is appropriate, the final price depends on pump type, location, pipe arrangement and whether alterations are required.

Stored-water systems

Many shower pumps are connected to stored hot and cold water systems. The wider tank and cylinder arrangement may affect performance.

Frequently asked questions

Does Ken include the cost of the new pump?

The initial range may not include a specific pump unit because pump type and specification need to be confirmed.

Can a noisy pump be repaired?

Sometimes the cause is installation or supply-related rather than the pump itself; diagnosis is the first step.
