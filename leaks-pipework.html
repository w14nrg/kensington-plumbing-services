Leak repair and pipework

Leak repair and pipework diagnosis in West London

Leaks range from a simple accessible compression joint to water appearing through a ceiling with the source hidden elsewhere. We start by identifying what can be seen, what can be isolated and how much access may be required.

Get a live estimateCall 020 7371 3333

Visible leaks

Accessible leaking pipes, fittings, flexi hoses and isolation valves can often be diagnosed and repaired during the same visit.

Hidden or unidentified leaks

Where the source is not obvious, the £75 visit covers the initial trace and diagnosis. Any significant opening-up work is discussed before it is carried out.

Active water leak

If water is escaping now and it is safe to do so, isolate the nearest affected valve or main stopcock. Keep clear of electrical fittings affected by water.

Frequently asked questions

Can Ken tell exactly where a hidden leak is?

No. Ken can help identify likely causes and price the initial diagnosis, but a hidden leak normally needs an on-site assessment.
