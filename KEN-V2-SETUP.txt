==========================

This V2 is the working flow build.

ken-schema.sql  D1 schema including appointment holds and confirmed bookings.

WHAT THE CUSTOMER FLOW DOES
---------------------------
1. Customer talks naturally to Ken.
2. OpenAI conversation identifies the likely job and asks relevant questions.
3. The 235-route KPS job system calculates an estimated range.
4. Customer clicks "See available appointments".
5. D1 returns genuinely available KPS online slots.
6. Customer selects one.
7. The slot is held for 35 minutes.
8. Customer enters name/mobile/email.
9. SumUp Hosted Checkout takes the £75 payment.
10. The return page verifies the SumUp checkout status server-side.
11. A successful payment changes the held appointment into a CONFIRMED booking.
12. The customer sees the confirmed appointment.
