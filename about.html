About Kensington Plumbing Services

A local plumbing business rebuilt around a better customer experience.

Kensington Plumbing Services is based in West London and focuses on practical plumbing repairs, water-side heating work and smaller drainage jobs for local homes and businesses.

Hands-on trade background

The business comes from a family background in the building and plumbing trades. That practical experience matters because real plumbing faults rarely fit perfectly into a website price list.

Why we built Ken

Most plumbing websites still ask you to call or fill in a form without giving any idea of price. Ken is designed to handle the first conversation immediately: understand what you can see, ask the questions that matter and run the likely job through a controlled pricing system.

Ken does not replace the plumber

A text conversation cannot confirm every fault. Ken helps you arrive at the visit with a clearer idea of the likely problem and likely cost. The plumber still makes the on-site assessment and confirms the exact repair price before additional work.
