Frequently asked questions

How does the £75 booking payment work?

The £75 covers attendance and diagnosis. When Kensington Plumbing Services carries out the repair, the £75 is credited against the final repair price. The exact price is confirmed on site before additional work proceeds.

Is Ken's estimate a fixed quote?

No. Ken gives an estimated price range based on the information supplied and the KPS pricing catalogue. The plumber confirms the actual job and exact price after an on-site assessment.

What areas do you cover?

The core service area is West London, including Kensington, West Kensington, Chelsea, Hammersmith, Fulham, Earl's Court, Barons Court and Shepherd's Bush.
