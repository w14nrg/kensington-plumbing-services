How it works

A clearer way to book a plumber.

Ken handles the first conversation. The KPS pricing engine handles the estimate. The plumber confirms the real job on site.

01

Describe it

Tell Ken exactly what you can see, hear or feel. You do not need plumbing terminology.

02

Answer a few questions

Ken asks about active leakage, access and postcode because these details can materially affect the job.

03

Get the estimate

The price comes from the KPS catalogue. AI is not allowed to invent the number.

04

Pay £75

Secure the attendance and diagnosis. The £75 is credited against completed repair work.

05

Choose your preferred slot

After successful payment, choose your preferred date and time window.

06

Confirm on site

The plumber checks the real fault and confirms the exact price before additional work proceeds.

Questions about the process

Why is the online price a range?

Access, parts and the true cause of the fault can only be confirmed on site. A range is more honest than pretending every repair has one fixed price.

What happens if the repair costs more than Ken estimated?

The plumber explains the actual findings and confirms the exact price before additional work is carried out.

What if I decide not to proceed after diagnosis?

The £75 covers the attendance and diagnosis already provided.
