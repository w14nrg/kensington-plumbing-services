About us

A local plumbing business, rebuilt around a better customer experience.

Kensington Plumbing Services is based in West London and focuses on practical plumbing repairs, water-side heating work and smaller drainage jobs for local homes and businesses.

The business comes from a family background in the building and plumbing trades stretching back through generations. The new Kensington.biz keeps that hands-on experience but changes the way customers first make contact.

Why we built Ken

Most plumbing websites still ask you to call, leave a form and wait, often without any idea of price. Ken is designed to do the first part of that conversation immediately: understand the problem, ask the useful questions and run the answers through a controlled pricing system.

Ken does not replace the plumber and does not pretend a text conversation is a final diagnosis. He helps you reach the on-site visit with a much clearer idea of the likely job and likely cost.

One important pricing rule

The £75 initial payment covers attendance and diagnosis. When we carry out the repair, that £75 is credited against the final repair price. If the real job is different from the online estimate, the exact price is confirmed on site before additional work proceeds.
