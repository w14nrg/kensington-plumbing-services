Tap and shower repair

Tap and shower repairs across West London

Taps and showers combine water pressure, valves, cartridges and sometimes pumps, so the symptom matters. Ken helps separate a simple fitting fault from a wider supply problem.

Get a live estimateCall 020 7371 3333

Dripping taps and valves

Common repairs include cartridges, washers, tap valves and leaking connections.

Shower temperature problems

Thermostatic cartridges, blocked filters and pressure imbalance can all affect shower temperature. The estimator treats these as diagnosis-led jobs.

Low pressure and pumps

Weak showers can be caused by the supply itself, filters or a shower pump. We avoid promising a pump replacement until the system is assessed.

Frequently asked questions

Can you replace a shower cartridge?

Yes, subject to identifying the correct cartridge and confirming that the existing valve is serviceable.

Do you work on electric showers?

Electrical work on an electric shower must be carried out by a competent person. Plumbing-side faults can still be assessed where appropriate.
