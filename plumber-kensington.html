Privacy

Privacy notice

This first-draft privacy notice should be reviewed before launch and updated with the business’s final legal entity details and data-retention policy.

Information collected

When you use Ken or request a booking, we may collect the messages you send, your postcode, estimate details, name, phone number, email address, payment reference and preferred appointment details.

Why we use it

We use this information to provide estimates, arrange appointments, diagnose and carry out requested work, process payments, prevent abuse and improve the service.

AI processing

Messages sent to Ken may be processed by an AI service to help produce conversational guidance. Prices are calculated separately by the Kensington Plumbing Services pricing engine.

Payments

Online payments are handled through SumUp. Kensington Plumbing Services does not need to store your full card details in this website.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact info@kensington.biz.
