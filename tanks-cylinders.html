Water tank and cylinder plumbing

Water tanks, overflows and hot-water cylinders

Overflowing loft tanks, failed float valves and cylinder leaks can waste water or cause significant damage if ignored.

Get a live estimateCall 020 7371 3333

Loft tank overflows

A running warning pipe often points to a failed or incorrectly adjusted float valve, although the source must be identified first.

Cylinder leaks

Some leaks come from valves or connections and are repairable. A failed cylinder itself requires a separate replacement quotation.

Access and safety

Loft access must be safe and workable. Ken's estimate assumes ordinary domestic access unless you tell him otherwise.

Frequently asked questions

Can you replace a loft tank ball valve?

Yes, provided the tank can be accessed safely.

Does a leaking cylinder always need replacing?

No. The leak may be from a connection or valve. The cylinder body needs assessment before replacement is recommended.
