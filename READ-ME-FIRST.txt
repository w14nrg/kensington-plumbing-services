EMAIL ALERTS
Tracking and the dashboard work without email.
Email alerts activate when either:
A) a Cloudflare Email Service binding called EMAIL is connected, or
B) a RESEND_API_KEY secret is added.

Also set:
NOTIFICATION_FROM_EMAIL = a verified sender, for example alerts@kensington.biz
OWNER_EMAIL is already set to info@kensington.biz in wrangler.jsonc.

Until email is connected, use the dashboard's browser alerts while it is open.
